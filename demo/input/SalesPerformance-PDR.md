# Sales Performance Dashboard - Product Dashboard Request

## Overview
A regional sales performance dashboard for the VP of Sales and regional managers. It should provide a quick overview of revenue, profit, and order KPIs, with the ability to drill down by region, product category, and customer segment. Updated monthly.

## KPIs
1. **Total Revenue** - Sum of all revenue, with comparison to target
2. **Total Profit** - Sum of all profit, with profit margin percentage
3. **Total Orders** - Count of orders, compared to target
4. **Average Order Value** - Revenue divided by number of orders

## Visualizations
1. **Revenue Trend** - Line chart showing monthly revenue over time, broken down by region
2. **Revenue vs Target by Region** - Bar chart comparing actual revenue to target per region
3. **Profit by Product Category** - Stacked bar or bar chart showing profit breakdown by category
4. **Top Customers** - Table showing top customers by revenue with their segment and NPS score

## Filters
- Date range picker (month selector)
- Region dropdown
- Product category dropdown
